Objective: get as close to 21 to beat the dealer.

  1. Player clicks HIT to add another card to hand.
  2. Player clicks STAND when their turn is done.
  3. Dealer (player 2) clicks HIT to add another card to hand.
  4. Dealer clicks STAND to end the round and see who won!
  5. Click Start to begin :)
